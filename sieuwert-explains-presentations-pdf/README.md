## Sieuwert explains ISO 27001 – presentations (PDF)

This directory contains short presentations from the “Sieuwert explains ISO 27001” series on the Youtube channel: [SieuwertExplains](https://www.youtube.com/@sieuwertexplains).

- **`episode-01-what-is-iso-27001.pdf`**: Introduces ISO 27001, its purpose, scope, and how it fits into information security management.
- **`episode-02-iso-27001-harmonized-structure.pdf`**: Explains the high-level “harmonized structure” (Annex SL) used in ISO management system standards and how ISO 27001 follows it.
- **`episode-03-organizational-controls.pdf`**: Walks through the organizational controls in ISO 27001 (e.g. policies, roles, processes) and their objectives.
- **`episode-04-people-controls.pdf`**: Covers people-related controls in ISO 27001, such as hiring, awareness, training, responsibilities, and disciplinary measures.

You can find a brief descriptin of each episode [here](https://ictinstitute.nl/sieuwertexplains/).
